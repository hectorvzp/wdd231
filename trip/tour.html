Oia Sunset Suites

Overview: Oia Sunset Suites in Santorini, Greece, is a luxury boutique hotel offering stunning views of the Caldera and the Aegean Sea. The hotel features an infinity pool with panoramic views, elegant suites with private terraces (some with hot tubs), and a perfect location to enjoy Santorini’s famous sunsets. Breakfast and concierge services are included. Ideal for couples and honeymooners, the hotel provides a romantic and picturesque stay. The average price is around $320 per night.

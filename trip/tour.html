Paris

Overview: Paris, the capital of France, is world-famous for its art, fashion, culture, and iconic landmarks. Known as “The City of Light,” it attracts millions of visitors each year with its romantic ambiance and historical charm. Top attractions include the Eiffel Tower, the Louvre Museum, Notre-Dame Cathedral, Montmartre with the Sacré-Cœur, and scenic Seine River cruises. The city is renowned for its cafés, patisseries, haute cuisine, and fashion scene, hosting Paris Fashion Week and countless designer boutiques. Spring and fall are the best times to visit for mild weather and fewer crowds, and public transportation makes exploring easy. Don’t miss trying classic French pastries, cheese, and wine—Paris offers a rich cultural and culinary experience that is unforgettable..

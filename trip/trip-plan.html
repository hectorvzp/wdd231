Site Plan: Come With Me Travels

1. Site Name and Purpose

Site Name

Come With Me Travels

The name is inviting, friendly, and suggests a partnership in travel planning, establishing a welcoming and personal tone, ideal for a service agency. The representative domain would be comewithmetravels.com.

Site Purpose

The website aims to be a complete resource hub for travelers, providing detailed information on **travel packages** (dynamically generated) and destinations, along with **hotel/resort data**, customer testimonials, and a direct contact channel for quotes. The goal is to convert interested visitors into booking clients.

2. Scenarios

These scenarios represent the main questions from the target audience and help prioritize content and functionality.

Scenario 1

"Which packages of 7 days or more are available for European destinations and what is the cheapest option?"

This drives the need for a tours page with **data filtering** and a clear display of **price** and **duration**, which will be the focus of the Array Method in JavaScript.

Scenario 2

"How can I quickly contact the agency to ask a question about travel insurance before finalizing my purchase?"

This drives the need for an **HTML Form** and clear contact information on the homepage, in addition to a possible Modal implementation for a contact or details pop-up.

3. Design and Style

Color Schema

The chosen palette aims to convey **trust**, **tranquility** (blue), and **enthusiasm/action** (orange/yellow).

#FFFFFF

#F48C06

Orange (Action)

  • Primary Blue (#0077B6): Headings (H1, H2), Primary Action Buttons, Active Links (Wayfinding).
  • White (#FFFFFF): Main content background, Cards, Text on Dark Backgrounds.
  • Light Cyan (#90E0EF): Subtle ` ` background, Divider Lines.
  • Orange (#F48C06): Secondary Buttons, Price/Offer Highlights, Link Hovers.

Typography

Playfair Display: Display Headings (H1, H2)

Roboto: Body Text, Navigation, General Readability (Standard)

  • **'Playfair Display', serif:** Used for titles and headings for an elegant and impactful touch.
  • **'Roboto', sans-serif:** Used for body text, ensuring high readability across all devices.

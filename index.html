About me

My name is Hector Zambrano, I’m 24 years old, originally from Venezuela, and currently living in Brazil. I’m deeply passionate about technology, innovation, and the transformative power of knowledge. From an early age, I’ve been drawn to challenges and personal growth — values I cultivated through sports, especially during my time practicing fencing. I bring that same discipline, focus, and drive into my journey as a software developer. I'm a naturally curious person, always eager to learn, improve, and contribute. I have a strong interest in video games, computer science, and the endless possibilities of the digital world. My ultimate professional goal is to become the best developer I can be, combining technical skill, creativity, and integrity to build meaningful, impactful solutions. Outside of work, I value family deeply — I am happily married, and I find strength, motivation, and purpose in the love and support of my wife.
